RImproving your Reading skills

Readingの受容性

教科書、雑誌、本、インタネット、映画の字幕、全部が分かる為にReadingが必要だと思う。 Reading is important for us to be able to understand almost everything out there -- textbooks, internet, games, song lyrics, and so forth. But how one is able to progress in their reading skill? The answer you are looking for is practice. At first, the Roman characters may seem too difficult to understand, but believe me, they are way easier than kanjis. The more you read the more your brain gets used to the language and eventually words will pop in your mind as you try to think in a different language, just like Japanese for you. The following are different sections, one for each level: beginner, intermediate and advanced.

Beginner: There is no other way to start other than textbooks. Good textbooks should give you a wide sense of daily conversation basics, as well as a solid grammar foundation to let you keep going in your self learning journey. Also, you should try to read book for children. They may have specific words for storytelling such as `Once upon a time`, but they contain a solid base of actual use of the grammar and words in their right context.
